How It Works

The Buddy Path

A synchronized progression tracking your milestones from dream to arrival.

01

Profile Discover

Analyzing academic profile, preferences, budgets, and choosing ideal destinations.

02

Buddy Match

Linking with top-tier universities and matching with student peers going to the same area.

Prep & Process

Curating Statement of Purpose (SOP), preparing recommendation letters, and university forms.

04

Fly & Land

Managing visa document checks, mock interviews, landing lodging, and campus networking.

Step 01

Assess Profile & Options

We kick off the journey by mapping out your profile. Select from hundreds of courses across the USA, UK, Canada, Europe, and Australia. Our buddy algorithms pinpoint the sweet spots matching your budget, grades, and dreams.

  • Detailed transcript & grades mapping
  • Country specific visa likelihood metrics
  • Financial plans & scholarship filtering
Step 02

Peer & University Match

Study abroad is never a solo mission. AddMeBuddy introduces you to peers targeting identical schools or destinations. Simultaneously, get direct application portals to top universities with fast-track offer evaluations.

  • Peer matching chat channels
  • Direct university application fast-track
  • Alumni connect interviews
Step 03

Document Drafting & Applications

Our experts and AI assistant polish your Statement of Purpose (SOP), refine your Resume, and organize Letters of Recommendation. We handle submission coordination to ensure zero application discrepancies.

  • Advanced SOP feedback & grammar polishing
  • Standardized test waiver consulting
  • Application timeline checklist trackers
Step 04

Visa Strategy & Landing Buddy

Get full preparation for crucial visa interviews. From financial document checklists to mock interviews with visa consultants. Once approved, connect with a local landing buddy for accommodation assistance and airport pick-up.

  • 1-on-1 mock visa interview rehearsals
  • Pre-departure luggage & checklist kits
  • Local accommodation mapping
